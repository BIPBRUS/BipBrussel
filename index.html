Mozaic
Global Tunes, Local Hearts

Mozaic is an non-profit organization based in Brussels with the ambition of enhancing social cohesion among diverse cultures. With our unique approach of combining and merging various cultures through dance and music, we aim to provide people with a fresh perspective on culture.

Music

We strive for a distinctive approach to music by seamlessly blending various cultures, creating an intriguing and diverse musical repertoire

Dance

Through our interactive dance workshop, we aim to encourage people to socialize in a unique manner.

Performance

After a couple of workshops there will be a performance. Everything that you've learned will be put on the test here.

Food

There can't be a fun evening without some nice food. Food will be provided by volunteers.

Team

We're a group of enthousiastic volunteers that wants to improve the interculturalism in Brussel

Grand Finale

The big party is starting! Once a year we will organise a party were everyone can show off their talent.
